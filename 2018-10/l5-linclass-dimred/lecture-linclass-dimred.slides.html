Постановка задачи¶

, $y=+1$: $$h(x,w) = p(y=+1|x,w) = \sigma(g(x))$$

$$ \mathcal{L}(w) = \prod_i^n h(x^{(i)},w)^{[y^{(i)} = +1]} (1 - h(x^{(i)},w))^{[y^{(i)} = -1]} \rightarrow \max_w$$ $$ -\log{\mathcal{L}(w)} = - \sum_i^n [y^{(i)} = +1]\cdot\log{(h(x^{(i)},w))} + {[y^{(i)} = -1]}\cdot\log{(1-h(x^{(i)},w))} \rightarrow \min_w$$ $$L(w) = \log{\mathcal{L}(w)} \rightarrow \min_w $$
